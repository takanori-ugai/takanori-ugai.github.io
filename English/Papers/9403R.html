BWNo. : UGAI-94-03-R
Title : Programming in the Small with Sushi
Authors : HORAI Hisayuki, UGAI Takanori
Publisher : ISIS Research Report
Language : Japanese

Abstract : A programming style for Sushi is presented by making the smallest programs and construct bigger programs. The essence of the programming style has been discussed in the field of stream programming, but we expect this paper to be a good textbook for Sushi programmers.
